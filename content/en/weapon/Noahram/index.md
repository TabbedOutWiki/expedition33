**Noahram** is a weapon usable by [Gustave](/character/gustave) and [Verso](/character/verso). It is Gustave's default weapon.

Like most default weapons, it has no passive abilities to unlock.

## Acquisition

Noahram is automatically acquired at the beginning of the game.

## Strategy

Noahram is the weakest weapon in its class due to its low stats and lack of passive abilities. It should be replaced with [Lanceram](/weapon/lanceram) as soon as possible.

## Trivia

- Noahram is named after [Noah](/character/gustave#trivia), the game's original protagonist. During development, this character was split into Gustave and Verso.
- Noahram is the only starter weapon that does not share a name with its user.
